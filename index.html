Our Mission

Transform locally available Lapsi into premium, globally loved products

LekBesy is a social-impact enterprise dedicated to transforming Nepali Hog Plum (Lapsi) into innovative products while empowering women and rural communities. We blend tradition with modern innovation to bring authentic Nepali flavors to the world.
