Welcome to LekBesy

Empowering Communities Through Premium Lapsi

Our Mission

Transform locally available Lapsi into premium, globally loved products

LekBesy is a social-impact enterprise dedicated to transforming Nepali Hug Plum (Lapsi) into innovative products while empowering women and rural communities. We blend tradition with modern innovation to bring authentic Nepali flavors to the world.

Learn Our Story

Why Choose LekBesy

Every LekBesy product is crafted with care, quality, and purpose. We're committed to preserving authentic Nepali traditions while creating value-added products that support local communities.
