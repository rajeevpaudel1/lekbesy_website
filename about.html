The Story Behind LekBesy

Our Beginning

A vision rooted in Nepal

LekBesy was founded with a simple yet powerful idea—to unlock the true potential of Lapsi, a fruit deeply connected to Nepali culture but often underutilized.

What started as an experiment has now become a growing social enterprise, working closely with rural communities and women to create products that carry both taste and purpose.

Founder & Vision

Dikshya Paudel

CEO & Co-Founder of LekBesy

LekBesy was built on a belief that local resources, when valued and nurtured, can create global impact. Dikshya Paudel founded LekBesy with a vision to transform Lapsi into premium products while uplifting communities.

Her focus is not just on building a successful brand, but on creating opportunities for women, promoting Nepali identity, and bringing authentic flavors of Nepal to the global stage.

“We are not just building products—we are building opportunities and pride in Nepali heritage.”
